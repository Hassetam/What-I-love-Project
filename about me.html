About Me

Hi, I'm Hasset. I love life most when it truly feels alive. I enjoy being around people, and I feel proud when I try new things. I started this project to share the things I love and the moments that make me say, “life is worth living.”

I'm a student, and this is my first website built with just HTML and CSS. I'd describe myself as having an eldest-daughter personality: caring, responsible, but also full of contradictions. I don't live in the middle ground. I can be loud or quiet, serious or funny, a perfectionist, or someone who just doesn't care.

People often meet the quiet version of me first, but give it time, and you'll discover the joy of the group.

Now that you know me a little better, explore the things I love and see what makes life exciting for me.
